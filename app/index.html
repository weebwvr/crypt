How to start investing
In order to withdraw funds, follow these steps:
  • First, you must select the wallet address where the funds will be transferred: this can be the address of an external exchange, local wallet or other service.
  • Open your investment unit card and find there the field for the withdrawal address, and next to it the "EDIT" button.
  • Once you have provided a withdrawal address, you need to confirm the withdrawal address by email. The address will change only after your confirmation.
